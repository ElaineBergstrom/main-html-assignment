Welsh Pembroke

Pembrokes are more commonly used in popular culture and media than that of the Cardigan .
The most famous example being Queen Elizabeth II having her own herd of pembrokes everywhere she went.

    Physical Characteristics of a Pembroke:
  1. Tails are more varied for Pembrokes
    • Docked tails are the more common practice
      Some countries forbid this practice, like Sweden.
    • Short tail stubs
    • Long tails are also possible but they tend to be thinner compared to the Cardigans.
  2. Smaller in size with less heavy bones
  3. Fur colors:
    • Red
    • Sable
    • Tri-colored (white, black, and tan)
  4. Smaller more pointed ears than that of the Cardigan
  5. Head and face shape is more fox like in appearance
